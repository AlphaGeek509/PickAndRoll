Cancellation Policy

Cancellations will be accepted only if there are not enough teams to fill a particular bracket, decided by the tournament director. If this occurs, you will receive a 100% refund. If a team registers past the entry deadline and is not admitted, they will receive a 100% refund.

F.A.Q.

Q: When is the tournament?
A: June 16-17, 2012. The tournament will only be held on Saturday if there are insufficient teams to require two days.

Q: When is the deadline?
A: Guaranteed mailed registration June 13th. Gauranteed online registration June 15th.

Mailed applications must be postmarked by June 13th to guarantee entry into the tournament. Entries postmarked after June 13th will be accepted at regular price without t-shirts or will require an additional $25 late fee with t-shirts until June 10th.

Q: How will I know if my team is in?
A: Once a team's entry has been processed, "Pick & Roll" will e-mail confirmation to the team captain.  This will serve as the team's official notification of acceptance into the tournament.  If a team does not provide a valid email, no confirmation will be received until the week of the tournament.

Q: How will I know when my team plays?
A: Brackets, rules, map, and other info. Will be emailed to all team captains beginning June 11th. Brackets will also be available online beginning June 11th. If you do not see your team name in your bracket or receive your info via email by June 1th, please contact our office immediately at (509) 499-4240.

Q: Do all team members have to be the same grade/age?
A: No. You can build your team however you like. Keep in mind however, that your team will play in the division of the oldest member.

Q: How much is the entry fee?
A: Open - $96
Open 6'1" & Under - $96
Open Co-ed (Adult & High School) - $96
High School - $96
Youth - $84

Q: Can our team make a player change?
A: Yes. Player changes may be made until your team checks in on Saturday. Player changes must be approved by the tournament director. The new player must fit into the criteria for the division of your team. (i.e. youth divisions, 6' & under, etc.)

Q: Can I play on more than one team?
A: While we do allow individuals to play on more than one team (teams must be in different brackets), we cannot control game schedules to guard against scheduling conflicts. Individuals playing on multiple teams could have multiple games scheduled at the same time.
