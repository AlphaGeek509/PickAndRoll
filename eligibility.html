All kinds of people play in Pick & Roll!

"Pick & Roll" is designed so that anyone can play!  Male, female, young, young at heart, short, tall, competitive or recreational players can all be a part of the action on the streets of downtown Medical Lake.  The youngest players must be entering third grade in the fall of 2011.  Other than that there is no age limit.   

Competition will be fierce on the Elite side of "Pick & Roll".  Playground legends, high school standouts, and college superstars will shine as they show their skills and play competitive basketball.

Whether you are on a competitive team out to win it all or just looking to get a little exercise with your friends, "Pick & Roll" is the place to get your game on!

All players must adhere to the "Pick & Roll" Code of Conduct: Athletics enhance physical, intellectual and emotional well-being.  They are an outlet for positive energy in a fun, competitive environment.  We invite you to play hard but play fair; enjoy the game regardless of the outcome; try your best and be proud of your efforts; respect your teammates and opponents and their performances; and recognize that all court officials and other volunteers are giving their best so that you can enjoy your experience.
