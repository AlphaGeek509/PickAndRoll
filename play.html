About the Tournament

“Pick & Roll” is a 3 on 3, outdoor, half-court, basketball tournament that is held annually every third Saturday in June, during Founder’s Day in Medical Lake, WA. This year the tournament will be held on June 15, 2013. Mailed applications must be postmarked by June 5, 2013 for guaranteed entry into the tournament. Online Registration open through June 7, 2013.

Tournament Info:

  • Tournament Day: June 15th, 2013 - ONE DAY ONLY
  • Teams may have 3-4 players.
  • All teams guaranteed at least 3 games.
  • All players receive an event t-shirt
  • Prizes awarded to 1st, 2nd and consolation winners.
  • Download Tournament Rules & Guidelines

Divisions

  • Male and Female Youth - Grades 3-8: Officials provided $92
  • Male and Female High School - Grades 9-12: Officials provided $100
  • Open CO-ED - Adult & High School combined: Call your own fouls $100
  • Male Open 6'1" and under - All team members must be 6'1" or under Call your own fouls $100
  • Male Open - Competitive Level of Play - Call your own fouls $100
